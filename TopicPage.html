Misrepresentation of Culture in Media: the Presentation!!

Who I am

I am a 18 year old Indian who has seen Indian people either rarely present in media or stereotyped.

Why I chose this topic

I watched the movie The Martian, and the book. I noticed that there was one huge difference. The Director of the Mars Missions was Venkat Kapoor, an Indian in the novels, but was called Vincent Kapoor and was played by a black actor in the film.

Why is it so common for mainstream Hollywood to take a representative role and whitewash/cast as a more mainstream race?
